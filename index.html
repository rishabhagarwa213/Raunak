chicken tikka

Chicken tikka is a popular Indian dish made from boneless chicken pieces marinated in a blend of yogurt and spices, including cumin, coriander, turmeric, and garam masala. The marinated chicken is skewered and grilled or baked until tender and slightly charred. Often served with mint chutney and onion slices, chicken tikka is known for its flavorful and smoky taste, making it a favorite appetizer or main course in many Indian restaurants.
